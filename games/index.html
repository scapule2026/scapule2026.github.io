Mobile Legends

Clash of Clans

Minecraft was first made public around May 2009 and was created by Markus "Notch" Persson. It has sold over 200 million copies worldwide and has amassed its great popularity and attention from its gameplay. It quickly became a childhood game of many people born in the late 2000's and early 2010's, and grew to be one of the most beloved topics of discussion in the gaming community.

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang was released by Moonton Games around July 2016, quickly spreading to the top of the mobile gaming industry in the Philippines. The game itself looked like a knockoff of another famous MOBA game, League of Legends, which still did not disrupt its popularity. However, its community grew to be even more toxic and erratic, which led to the dismay of much of the community.

Clash of Clans was released by Supercell around August 2012 on IOS, and eventually on October 2013 for Android. It used to be one of the most popular mobile games around the 2010's, but it declined slowly after the lack of updates and the fact that more mobile games were being produced. Nowadays, it sees more frequent updates from its creators, which will hopefully lead to their comeback.
