Introduction to Video Games

The history of video games is quite unique. In the 80's and 90's, all they had were arcades and consoles. Nowadays, there are desktops, mobile devices, etc., as well as a broader list of genres. But why? Why play video games? What does this provide that is better than plaaying real life games? Well, it is mainly the variety and accessibility of video games that makes it popular. Most people that play video games choose to do it instead of real-life activities because it is simply easier to do. Because of the current technology, playing games online have been way easier than in the past, where there were limited computers and phones available to the market.
